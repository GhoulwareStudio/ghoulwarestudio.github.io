WHO ARE WE?

Ghoulware is a 2-man Belgian indie game studio based near Mons.
We love making sci-fi games.
Alongside producing our own games, we also offer freelancing services in both programming and asset production.

OUR SERVICES

Software Development

We're experienced in both software, web, and game development. Our engines of choice are Godot and Unity. Our tools of the trade are C#, React and React Native.

Asset Production

An extensive knowledge of the 3D production pipeline to create game-ready assets tailor-made for your projects, for any engine or use case.
